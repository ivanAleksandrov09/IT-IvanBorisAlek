Trip Planer

The Land Transportation Planner is a web application designed to help you calculate routes and estimate
costs for land transportation, including driving, cycling, and walking. The application integrates with
mapping services to provide accurate routes and includes features such as calculating fuel costs for car
travel, estimating toll taxes, and suggesting rest stops for long journeys.
